Tournament Rules

Key guidelines to ensure fair and enjoyable competition

Time Controls

Classical: 90+30, Rapid: 25+10, Blitz: 5+3. Clocks provided for all games.

Sportsmanship

Respect opponents and officials. Zero tolerance for unsportsmanlike conduct.

Device Policy

Phones must be silenced and stored. No electronic assistance during play.

Touch-Move Rule

Standard FIDE rules apply. If you touch a piece, you must move it if legal.
