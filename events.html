How to Register for Events

1

Check Event Details

Review event information, dates, times, and requirements. Make sure the event matches your skill level and schedule.

2

Register Online

Click the "Register Now" or "Learn More" button on the event. Fill out the registration form with your details.

3

Make Payment

Pay any required fees securely online. Members enjoy significant discounts on most events.

4

Receive Confirmation

Get email confirmation with event details, location instructions, and what to bring.
