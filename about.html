About Our Organization

Learn about our mission, values, and the community we've built for chess enthusiasts

Our Story

Building a Community of Chess Enthusiasts

Founded in 2023, Home of Chess Players began as a small gathering of local chess enthusiasts who wanted to create a welcoming space for players of all skill levels. What started as a weekly meetup at a local coffee shop has grown into a vibrant community with hundreds of members.

Our mission has always been clear: to make chess accessible to everyone while fostering a competitive yet friendly environment where players can grow, learn, and connect with others who share their passion for the game.

Today, we organize regular tournaments, workshops, and social events that bring together beginners, casual players, and seasoned competitors from all walks of life.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To create an inclusive community where chess enthusiasts of all skill levels can learn, compete, and grow together through organized events, educational resources, and social interaction.

Our Vision

To become the leading chess community that bridges the gap between casual play and competitive chess, making the game more accessible and enjoyable for everyone.

Our Values

We believe in inclusivity, sportsmanship, continuous learning, and the power of community. Every move matters, both on and off the board.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our organization

Do I need to be an experienced player to join?

Not at all! We welcome players of all skill levels, from complete beginners to experienced competitors. We have resources and events tailored for everyone.

What are the membership fees used for?

Membership fees help us organize events, maintain our facilities, develop educational resources, and provide tournament prizes. We're a non-profit organization, and all funds are reinvested into the community.

How often do you hold tournaments?

We host monthly tournaments for members, plus special championship events quarterly. We also organize casual play nights every week for those who prefer a less competitive environment.

Can children join the organization?

Yes! We have a junior program for players under 16, with age-appropriate tournaments and learning materials. Players under 13 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian at events.

Do you offer online membership or events?

Yes, we offer virtual memberships with access to online tournaments, video lessons, and our digital community forum. This is perfect for those who can't attend in-person events regularly.
