Introduction to Chess Openings

1,240 views June 15, 2024 Beginner 15 minutes

Instructor: GM Alex Morgan

Chess Grandmaster with 15+ years of teaching experience. Former national champion.

Description

In this beginner-friendly lesson, GM Alex Morgan introduces the fundamental principles of chess openings. You'll learn:

  • Control the center of the board
  • Develop your pieces efficiently
  • Castle early for king safety
  • Avoid moving the same piece multiple times in the opening
  • Common opening mistakes to avoid

This lesson is perfect for players who want to build a solid foundation for their opening repertoire.
