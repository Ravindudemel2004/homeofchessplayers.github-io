Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference. When downgrading, the changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a contract or commitment period?

No, all our plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime. The annual plan is prepaid for one year but still comes with our 30-day money-back guarantee.

Do you offer family or group discounts?

Yes! We offer family plans and group discounts for schools, clubs, or organizations with 5+ members. Contact our membership team for custom pricing.

How do I cancel my membership?

You can cancel anytime from your account settings. If you cancel within 30 days of joining, you'll receive a full refund. After 30 days, cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing period.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees. The price you see is what you pay. Tournament entry fees for special events are separate but always clearly listed with at least 50% discount for members.

Still have questions? Contact our support team for assistance.
